What is Refucation?

Why trust us?

Due to the situation in Ukraine, many students and children have been displaced from their education in a blink of an eye. As a result, they have left to fend for themselves with no direction of what to study. Given that the student has access to the internet, Refucation provides students and teachers the opportunity to reconnect even after their school may have been shut down. Through us, students can start learning and teachers can resume teaching with a payment incentive provided by NGO donations and a UN sub-comittee will be encouraged to established to aid us.
